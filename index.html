Among Us

Among Us is an multiplayer mobile/PC game from Innersloth. There are ten "crewmates" on the ship that need to finish tasks, but there's a catch. Among those ten crewmates is one "imposter" who sets out on an ambitious mission to kill them all and sabatoge their ship. It's up to the crewmates to vote out the imposter and save their lives. Are you ready?

Among Us Mods

Several custom "mods" were made for the game, with special game modes including "Jester", "Sheriff", and even "Cyborg". They were created by a developer who goes by 'Ottomated'. His GitHub can be found here.
